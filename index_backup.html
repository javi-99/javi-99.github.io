Javed Ahmad

Computer Vision Researcher

Hi, I am Javed , a Computer Vision and Robotics researcher working in industry, specializing in SLAM, 3D mapping, and multimodal scene understanding.
My work focuses on building end-to-end 3D perception pipelines for autonomous systems, spanning UAV platforms, robotic manipulators, and large-scale reconstruction.
Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at IIT, contributing to 3D Gaussian Splatting SLAM for immersive teleoperation (ADVR)
and developing autonomous robotic 3D scanning systems for cultural-heritage digitization (CCHT). I completed my Ph.D. at PAVIS-IIT,
where I worked on LiDAR-camera fusion and multi-view 3D object localization

Bio

Hi,
I am a Postdoctoral Researcher currently working on Gaussian Splatting based SLAM system at ADVR, IIT, Genova.. Previously, I was also Posdoc and worked on 3D scanning/reconstruction of artefacts using robotic manipulators at CCHT, IIT, Venice. I did my PhD at the PAVIS Lab, IIT, with Alessio Del Bue, where I specialized in 3D object detection and multimodal fusion for enhanced 3D scene perception. I'm always open to collaboration on research and projects that align with mutual goals. Feel free to reach out.

Projects

CTE-Genova

Developing an automated platform for the scanning and digital preservation of cultural heritage artifacts, as part of the Genova-based House of Emerging Technologies project. This system utilizes robotic automation to enable comprehensive 3D scans of valuable objects, supporting their study and preservation.
