Bio

I am Ph.D. student at PAVIS Lab, iit , where I am working in 3D scene understanding with geometric and deep learning reasoning. My research interests lies in 3D objects localization and detection from single or multimodalities. Currently working on multimodal fusion strategy - a new way of learning to represent modalities information at feature level to complement sensors (image and LiDAR) deficiencies.
