Bio

Hi,
I am a Postdoctoral Researcher currently working on 3D reconstruction using robotic manipulators at CCHT, IIT, Italy. I completed my PhD at the PAVIS Lab, IIT, where I specialized in 3D object detection and multimodal fusion for enhanced 3D scene perception. I'm always open to collaboration on research and projects that align with mutual goals. Feel free to reach out.
